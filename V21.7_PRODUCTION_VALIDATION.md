## Payback sorting validation

Synthetic regression rows covered:

- immediate payback;
- 2-year, 10-year and 70-year positive payback;
- no positive EBITDA;
- CAPEX missing;
- actual data missing.

Validated ordering:

- Best to worst: Immediate → 2 years → 10 years → 70 years → No payback → unknown values.
- Worst to best: No payback → 70 years → 10 years → 2 years → Immediate → unknown values.

Unknown rows remain at the bottom in both directions and are secondarily sorted by site name.
