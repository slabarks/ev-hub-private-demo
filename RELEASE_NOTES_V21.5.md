# V21.5 release notes

## Portfolio Financial Performance investor-table refinement

V21.5 implements the agreed board-facing simplification without changing the audited Next 12m forecast methodology.

### Funding

- SuperValu Tipperary is now a confirmed LDV3 funding match.
- Exact record: SuperValu, Kickham Place, Tipperary, E34 VP78.
- Confirmed funding: €79,870.
- The record is eligible for the normal portfolio-wide funding action and no longer requires individual review.

### Actual & Next 12m kWh

- Replaced the dense paragraph with two vertically stacked cards.
- The first card shows the Next 12m forecast and the increase/decrease versus the comparable actual basis.
- The second card shows trailing-12-month actuals for mature sites, or actual delivered kWh, observed days and annualised basis for younger sites.
- The forward calibrated model value was removed from the main cell and remains available in the detailed forecast audit.

### Actual vs age-matched model

- Renamed the column to `Actual vs age-matched model`.
- Replaced the dense block with two cards: model-performance percentage/status and the actual/model evidence over the same operating period.
- Standardised investor terminology to `Actual above model`, `In line with model`, `Actual below model`, and `Early evidence`.
- Updated filters, portfolio summary cards and PDF reporting to use the same historical age-matched interpretation.

### Energy & network

- Retained the two-card layout.
- Simplified the network card to show annual standing + capacity cost and the selected MIC only.
- Full standing and capacity-rate detail remains available in the hover explanation and calculation definitions.

## Unchanged

- Next 12m forecast engine and 1% phased traffic-growth assumption.
- Rolling-30-day, daily and monthly forecast audit graph.
- CAPEX variance direction and funding impact on net returns.
- Browser-local historical-data upload.
