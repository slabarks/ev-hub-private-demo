# V17.9 Portfolio Financials revenue label fix

- Revenue/year in Portfolio Financial Performance is no longer labelled simply as actual unless a site has at least 365 operational days.
- Sites below 365 days show projected/projected estimated annual run-rate.
- Sites with at least 365 days show actual T12M or estimated T12M depending on revenue source.
- Revenue cells include hover tooltip explaining the basis.
- Dashboard wording updated to clarify next-year revenue is projected unless 365+ days are available.
