## Real browser workflow

The actual application was tested through Chromium at 1,600 px, 1,366 px and 1,200 px widths.

Validated:

- 37 financial rows and all ten columns render before and after upload;
- no generated top scrollbar exists;
- all ten columns are visible without a horizontal scrollbar at 1,600 px;
- all ten column headers remain visible at 1,366 px with no scrollbar UI;
- below 1,280 px, one controlled bottom scrollbar is used;
- the supplied ZIP activates uploaded actuals for 37/37 active sites;
- browser status reports the canonical source and server processing time;
- forecast modal opens and renders the rolling-30-day chart;
- funding modal opens and shows net invested CAPEX controls;
- no page errors or browser console errors occurred.

## Financial integrity

- Gross CAPEX variance remains model day-one CAPEX versus gross actual day-one CAPEX.
- Funding affects net invested CAPEX and effective returns only when applied.
- EBITDA reconciles revenue minus energy, standing/capacity and other OPEX.
- The matrix and forecast graph use the same Next 12m forecast result.

## Deployment requirement

V21.1 must be deployed as a complete package. A mixed V21/V21.1 frontend and backend is intentionally rejected by the upload preflight to prevent the incomplete-response error observed in the previous deployment.
