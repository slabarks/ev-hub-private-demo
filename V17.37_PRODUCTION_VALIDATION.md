## Forecast controls

- Empirical status requires at least three suitable mature-site profiles and at least six maturity months supported by two or more mature sites.
- Stable mature profiles are preferred when at least three are available.
- Insufficient evidence activates the conservative prior rather than presenting unsupported precision.
- Current, next-12-month and mature annual values remain separate in the investor table.
- Revenue ranges are confidence-adjusted and constrained so downside ≤ base ≤ upside.
- Site and portfolio payback use cumulative monthly EBITDA proxy, not CAPEX divided by one immature annual run rate.

## Deployment acceptance

V17.37 is suitable for production deployment subject to the normal operational requirement that the latest charger-level daily export is uploaded when investors expect an empirical maturity curve. Without that upload, the system remains operational but transparently uses the conservative prior.
